Writing an Essay

 

Write an essay about what your future life will be in comparison with your present.

 

I.

 

Consider the following plan to write your essay.

 

Introduction 
Use a question or a phrase to engage your readers.

 

Paragraph 1 
Give your strongest point of view and explanation.

 

Paragraph 2 
Give a second point of view, explanation and examples.

 

Paragraph 3 
If possible, give a contrasting view about the same topic.

 

Conclusion  
Make a summary of your thesis and give your final opinion about the topic.
